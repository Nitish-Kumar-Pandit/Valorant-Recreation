WE ARE VALORANT

Blend your style and experience on a global, competitive stage. You have 13 rounds to attack and defend your side using sharp gunplay and tactical abilities. And, with one life per-round, you'll need to think faster than your opponent if you want to survive. Take on foes across Competitive and Unranked modes as well as Deathmatch and Spike Rush.

RAZE

RAZE

Raze explodes out of Brazil with her big personality and big guns. With her blunt-force-trauma playstyle, she excels at flushing entrenched enemies and clearing tight spaces with a generous dose of "boom."

PHOENIX

PHOENIX

Hailing from the U.K., Phoenix's star power shines through in his fighting style, igniting the battlefield with flash and flare. Whether he's got backup or not, he'll rush into a fight on his own terms.

REYNA

REYNA

Forged in the heart of Mexico, Reyna dominates single combat, popping off with each kill she scores. Her capability is only limited by her raw skill, making her highly dependent on performance.

Download now and compete globally to elevate your skills and dominate the battlefield!

Valorant is a tactical shooter by Riot Games, featuring 5v5 gameplay with unique Agents who have special abilities. Players engage in strategic gunfights and team-based objectives in various game modes, blending traditional shooter mechanics with character-based skills.
